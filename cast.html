Directors and Producers

The Dirt is directed by Jeff Tremaine, produced by Allen Kovac, Erik Olsen, Julie Yorn, Rick Yorn. Lastly, the screen play is done by Rich Wilkes and Amanda Adelson.



Star Actors

The Dirt has many prominent actors such as Machine Gun Kelly (Tommy Lee), Pete Davidson (Tom Zutaut), Douglas Booth (Nikki Sixx), and Daniel Webber (Vince Neil). Unfortunately, there are no leading actresses in this movie.
